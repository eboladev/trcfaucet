What is Terracoin?

Terracoin (TRC) is a digital currency, based on Bitcoin. It operates on a peer to peer network, the same technology behind Skype and BitTorrent. Futhermore, Terracoin is completely decentralized, meaning it is not controlled by any government or central bank.
